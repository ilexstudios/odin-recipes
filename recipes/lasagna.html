Ingredients

  • 1 pound Pork Sasuage
  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 4 Cups Water
  • 3 Cans of Black Beans
  • 1 Can Diced Tomato
Steps
  1. Cook and Stir the Sasuage and ground beef together.
  2. Bring the soup to a boil, reduce heat to a simmer, cover and simmer for 45 minutes.
